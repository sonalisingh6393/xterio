BAGUETTE SKIN

Solar Protection and Style with Our Terracotta Facades

Our glazed terracotta facades are the ideal choice for providing efficient solar protection and a touch of elegant architectural design to buildings of any size. Whether it’s to combat the scorching heat or enhance the aesthetics of your building, our ceramic facades are the perfect solution.

Versatility in Orientation

Our facades are designed with exceptional versatility. They adapt to any orientation, whether horizontal or vertical, making them highly adaptable to the specific needs of your building.

Customisation of Colours and Finishes

We offer a wide range of natural and glazed colours with both gloss and matt finishes. In addition, we have the capability to produce custom finish colours to your requirements. This gives you the flexibility to achieve the precise aesthetic your project demands.

Technical and aesthetic advantages

Our terracotta facades offer numerous technical and aesthetic advantages for your facade:

  • Durable Colours: Terracotta colours and glazes remain unalterable over time.
  • Self-cleaning: They are automatically cleaned by rain, reducing the need for maintenance.
  • Durable Resistance: Designed to resist wear and tear over the years.
  • Energy Saving: Reduce energy costs by 25 to 40% and enjoy greater thermal and acoustic comfort.
  • Waterproofing: Keep your building watertight thanks to our screens. Passive Ventilation: Encourage passive ventilation for a healthier and more energy efficient environment.
  • Thermal Insulation: Contributes to thermal insulation and indoor comfort.
  • Easy Replacement: Our system allows for piece-by-piece replacement, making repairs easier.
  • Value for your Property: Add value to your property with these high quality solar screens.
