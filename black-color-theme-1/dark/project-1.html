Premium Solutions for Modern Facades

Frontek porcelain exterior tiles are a revolutionary choice for ventilated façades, designed to meet the highest standards of durability, sustainability, and aesthetics. These advanced tiles provide superior performance against harsh climatic conditions, combining high strength, low water absorption, and lightweight design. Whether you're upgrading outdoor floor tiles or seeking exterior tiles for doorsteps, Frontek offers unmatched versatility and reliability.

Why Frontek Tiles are the Best Outdoor Tiles





01

Exceptional Durability


Frontek tiles are crafted from high-quality porcelain using advanced extrusion techniques. Their double-walled design enhances structural integrity, making them the best outdoor tiles for both residential and commercial projects.

02

Thermal Efficiency


With outstanding thermal insulation properties, these tiles regulate interior temperatures, reducing energy consumption and creating eco-friendly solutions for modern buildings.

03

Minimal Maintenance


Frontek outdoor wall tiles are easy to clean and maintain, ensuring long-term cost savings without compromising their aesthetic appeal. Their surfaces resist wear and tear in all types of traffic areas.

04

Water and Weather Resistant


Designed with minimal water absorption, Frontek tiles withstand extreme weather, making them perfect exterior tiles for doorsteps, walls, and facades.

05

Acoustic Comfort


Reduce noise pollution with Frontek’s superior acoustic insulation, creating a peaceful environment indoors and out.

06

Lightweight and Stylish

These lightweight tiles are available in a variety of designs, offering the best combination of elegance and functionality for outdoor floor tiles or wall applications.

Versatile Applications of Frontek Tiles

Frontek porcelain tiles are ideal for:

Outdoor Floor Tiles:

Add sophistication and durability to patios, walkways, and more.

Outdoor Wall Tiles:

Create stunning facades that stand out with contemporary style.

Exterior Tiles for Doorsteps:

Ensure strength and elegance in high-traffic entrance areas.

The Benefits of Choosing Frontek

Sustainable Design

Frontek tiles support energy-efficient construction with ventilated façade systems that enhance insulation, leading to up to 40% energy savings.

Precision and Flexibility

Engineered for seamless installation, these tiles can be easily cut to precision on-site, offering design flexibility without compromising durability.

Innovative Installation System

Frontek uses advanced horizontal aluminum profiles to securely anchor tiles while enabling multi-level layering for added visual depth.

Elevate Your Exteriors with Frontek



Frontek porcelain exterior tiles are the ultimate choice for architects, designers, and developers looking to combine innovation, style, and performance. From best outdoor tiles to sophisticated outdoor wall tiles, Frontek provides tailored solutions for every project.
