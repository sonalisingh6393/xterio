ORGANIC LOUVER

Reinventing ceramic decoration

We present a new and innovative product. A piece that combines new technologies and traditional thinking to create a different and modern concept. Architecture and design have always grown together, sharing spaces and projects. However, both would be nothing without those who make them progress through the generations.

This is Organic Louver, Tempio’s new line of ceramic louvers that fully fuses architecture and design, catering to contemporary and more alternative needs. Organic Louver is born from emotional designs that seek a timeless perspective that has a place in any space.

Infinite combinations

Each piece of Organic Louver will be art, offering a look guided by geometric, sinuous or pointed shapes, which can be found cladding facades of all kinds or as decorative material, offering elegant and alternative solutions for room dividers, facade sunshades or ventilated building envelopes. Thus, whether incorporated into indoor environments or outdoor installations, our new Organic Louver range is a satisfying puzzle that anyone can play with in terms of design. Ingenuity and creativity guide the projects in which this new artistic weapon is included, making more visible than ever the capacity we have at Tempio to tackle projects of any magnitude and the work we do in conjunction with architects and/or designers.

Unique Pieces

Each piece of Organic Louver will be art, offering an appearance guided by geometric, sinuous or pointed shapes, which can be found cladding facades of all kinds or as decorative material, offering elegant and alternative solutions for room dividers, facade sunshades or ventilated building cladding.

Innovation

Organic Louver is Tempio’s innovative ceramic line, fully merging architecture with design to fulfill both contemporary and alternative needs. Designed with emotional inspiration, Organic Louver brings a timeless touch to any space, offering versatile solutions for modern architecture.
