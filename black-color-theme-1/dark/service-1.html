Our Services

01

Systexx Glass Fabric for Walls & Ceilings

Systexx offers premium glass fabric solutions for walls and ceilings that combine aesthetics and functionality. With pre-glued, crack-bridging fabrics and designer finishes, it ensures durability, fire resistance, and effortless application, making it a versatile choice for various surfaces.

02

Unique Façade Expressions

STENI’s advanced façade panels redefine architectural creativity with customizable colors, surfaces, and textures. Designed for durability and sustainability, these easy-to-maintain panels resist harsh weather, ensuring vibrant and lasting exteriors with a low environmental impact.

03

Porcelain and Terracotta Panels

Tempio’s porcelain and terracotta panels blend elegance and durability, offering artistic façade solutions like baguettes and antique finishes. With energy-efficient features and endless customization options, they enhance building aesthetics while standing the test of time.

04

Magnetic and Functional Walls

Transform walls with Systexx’s magnetic and whiteboard capabilities, ideal for creative and functional applications in offices and schools. These durable solutions turn ordinary walls into interactive and productive spaces.
