ORGANIC LOUVER

Reinventing Ceramic Decoration

Introducing a groundbreaking product that blends advanced technology with traditional concepts to create a modern, distinctive design. Architecture and design have always evolved together, sharing spaces and projects. Yet, both rely on innovation to move forward through the generations.

Organic Louver is Tempio’s new line of ceramic louvers that seamlessly combines architecture with design, meeting the demands of contemporary and alternative spaces. With emotional designs, Organic Louver offers a timeless perspective that can fit seamlessly into any environment.

Infinite Combinations

Each Organic Louver piece becomes a work of art, featuring geometric, sinuous, or pointed shapes. These versatile pieces can be used for cladding facades, decorative elements, room dividers, sunshades, or ventilated building envelopes. Whether used indoors or outdoors, Organic Louver allows for endless design possibilities, offering elegant and innovative solutions.

Unique Pieces

Every piece of Organic Louver is crafted to be a unique work of art, with shapes that range from geometric to sinuous to pointed. These pieces serve as striking facades or decorative materials, adding sophistication and originality to projects like room dividers, sunshades, or ventilated building cladding.

Innovation

Organic Louver is Tempio’s innovative ceramic line, fully merging architecture with design to fulfill both contemporary and alternative needs. Designed with emotional inspiration, Organic Louver brings a timeless touch to any space, offering versatile solutions for modern architecture.
