Commitment to quality

Every piece undergoes stringent quality controls throughout the entire production process. Our laboratory conducts comprehensive tests, trials, and checks to ensure the excellence of our products.

Tests include assessments of mechanical strength, thermal shock resistance, chemical resistance of glaze, purity of components, and stability of forms.

In essence, we implement a comprehensive quality control system from clay selection to the final packaging to guarantee that each product is in impeccable condition.
