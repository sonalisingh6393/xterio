BRICK SKIN

Sustainable and Highly Resistant Ceramic Facade

Brick Skin is a unique collection that stands out as an exceptional solution for facades and ceramic cladding. The production method and the natural raw materials used make these pieces a durable product, resistant to thermal shock, erosion, chemicals, fire, and that retains its color over time, among other advantages.

This collection is presented as a resounding bet for those who seek to maintain a traditional but daring touch in the architecture of their facades. Breaking with routine, Brick Skin is a sustainable and avant-garde choice that responds to the bioclimatic needs of the future.
